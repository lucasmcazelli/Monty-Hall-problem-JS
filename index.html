Monty Hall Simulator

The Monty Hall problem is a famous probability puzzle. You pick one of several doors, behind one of which is a car and behind the others are goats. After your pick, Monty, who knows what's behind each door, opens another door to reveal a goat. You then get the chance to stick with your original choice or switch to another door. The question is: Is it in your best interest to switch or not?

This simulation demonstrates the Monty Hall problem by running multiple rounds and updating the results in real-time. One simulation will stick with the original door choice, and the other will switch.
